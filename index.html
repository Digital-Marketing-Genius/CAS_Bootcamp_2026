About the Bootcamp

Not just a workshop. A launchpad.

CAS Boot Camp is an intensive, two-day, non-residential program designed to empower and equip young people between the ages of 14-22 with the knowledge and tools required for life and their future careers.

For a decade, CAS Bootcamp has helped hundreds of young people discover their abilities, grow the right competencies, and express those gifts through career, enterprise, leadership, and service. Over the years, our guest speakers have been drawn from top rated organizations such as Samsung, Air France KLM, Deloitte, WEMA Bank, PUNCH Newspapers, Flutterwave, Mobil Producing, Background Check International, Main One, and Interswitch.

This 10th edition would feature specialized masterclasses (Tech, Creatives and Business), career panels, keynotes, gamified learning and values-based activities.

CAS Bootcamp bridges the gap between aspiration and direction, helping young people prepare not only for the workforce but for life as impactful and principled members of society.

Come ready for career clarity, values-based mentoring, peer connection, and practical next steps you can apply immediately.
